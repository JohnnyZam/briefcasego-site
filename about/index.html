About BriefcaseGo

Knowledge on the go.

No briefcase required.
A small, independent studio building focused study and learning apps for people who want to make use of spare moments — not manage another subscription.

The Idea

Built by someone who
was studying too.

BriefcaseGo started with a practical problem: studying for a tough professional certification exam while juggling a full-time job, with no reliable way to review material on a commute, in a waiting room, or on a flight without an internet connection or yet another app demanding an account.

The first product — the AAISM™ Study App — was built to solve exactly that. It's a single, self-contained file: no login, no subscription, no syncing, no ads. Open it in any browser and it works, online or off, on the first try and the hundredth.

Download once. Own it forever. Study anywhere.

That same philosophy is now the foundation for everything BriefcaseGo builds — whether it's certification prep, trivia, or other on-the-go learning tools for curious minds.

How We Build

A few rules we
don't break.

Every BriefcaseGo product is held to the same short list of standards.

01
Offline First
If it needs a constant internet connection to be useful, it's not a BriefcaseGo product.
02
No Accounts
Nothing to sign up for, nothing to remember a password for. Buy it, download it, use it.
03
Buy Once
No subscriptions, no recurring charges, no feature paywalls added after purchase.
04
Original Content
Every question, definition, and explanation is written from scratch — never lifted from official materials.
